Class JobChart Grade 9

The jobchart for grade 9!

Monitor
Maintains class discipline and assists the teacher in managing classroom activities.
Class Ambassador
Represents the class in school events and acts as a bridge between students and teachers.
Register Manager
Takes daily attendance and ensures records are updated accurately.
Classroom Decorator
Designs and maintains creative displays and decorations in the classroom.
Notebook Collector
Collects and distributes notebooks and classwork efficiently.
Tech Support
Assists in handling classroom technology like projectors and computers.
Hygiene Hero
Ensures cleanliness in the classroom and promotes hygiene practices among classmates.
